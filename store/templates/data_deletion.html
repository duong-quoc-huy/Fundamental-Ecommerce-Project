Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: December 21, 2024

Important Notice

Deleting your account is a permanent action and cannot be undone. All your data, including your profile information, purchase history, and preferences will be permanently removed from our systems.

How to Delete Your Account and Data

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your account at firefly-ecommerce.duckdns.org
  2. Navigate to your Account Settings or Profile page
  3. Scroll down to the "Account Management" section
  4. Click on "Delete Account" or "Delete My Data" button
  5. Read the warning message carefully
  6. Confirm the deletion by entering your password
  7. Click "Permanently Delete My Account" to finalize

Alternative Method

If you're unable to access your account or prefer manual assistance, you can request data deletion by contacting us directly:

Email:

support@firefly-ecommerce.com

Subject Line:

Data Deletion Request - [Your Email Address]

Required Information:

Please include the following in your request:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your full name (as registered)
  • Reason for deletion (optional)
  • Confirmation that you understand this action is permanent

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, we will permanently remove the following information:

  • Your profile information (name, username, bio)
  • Your email address and contact details
  • Your phone number
  • Your profile picture and uploaded images
  • Your order history and preferences
  • Your saved items and wishlists
  • Your reviews and comments
  • Connected social media accounts (Google, Facebook)
  • Any other data associated with your account

What We May Retain

For legal and security purposes, we may retain certain information:

  • Transaction records (required for tax and accounting purposes for 7 years)
  • Records of refunds or disputes (required by law)
  • Information necessary to prevent fraud or abuse
  • Anonymous analytics data that cannot identify you

Note: All retained data is kept in accordance with applicable privacy laws and regulations.

Processing Time

Data deletion requests are typically processed within the following timeframes:

  • Self-service deletion: Immediate (within 24 hours)
  • Email requests: Within 7-14 business days
  • Complete data removal: Within 30 days maximum

You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been successfully deleted.

Before You Delete

Please consider these alternatives before permanently deleting your account:

  • Deactivate temporarily: Hide your account without losing your data
  • Update privacy settings: Control what information is visible
  • Unsubscribe from emails: Stop receiving newsletters without deleting your account
  • Download your data: Export a copy of your data before deletion

Contact Us

If you have any questions about data deletion or this policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@firefly-ecommerce.com
  • Website: firefly-ecommerce.duckdns.org

Privacy Policy: For more information about how we handle your data, please read our Privacy Policy.
